FAQs

What does this app exactly do?
Our platform offers a variety of features to help you learn about recycling, including

A section that explains how to recycle at home - Our easy-to-understand guide will teach you how to separate your waste into different categories and properly dispose of them.

A section that shows nearby recycling centres - Our interactive map will help you find the nearest recycling centres in your area, complete with contact information.

A section that explains general information on recycling - Learn about the benefits of recycling, the recycling process and the different types of materials that can be recycled.

A section on how to become an agent - Our guide will provide information on how to become an agent and help you understand the role of agents in the recycling process.
How can I add my organisation as a location on your Recycling Locator?
We are able to accept applications from other organisations that have recycling or reuse locations available that are open to the public. For more details, please email info@howtorecycle.ng
How can I find out how to recycle a specific item?
Navigate: Home → Recycle at home → select the item you have.

Our Recycle at home page covers a lot of items and gives details on whether you can recycle them at home or at out-of-home drop-off points.
The information about a recycling bank or Recycling Centre on the Recycling Locator is incorrect, how can I report this?
Please send us a mail at: info@howtorecycle.ng
How do we install your QR on our product?
On our home page → we have the option to download our QR code → you can just download the QR from here and place them on your products. For more info: contact → info@howtorecyle.ng
How do I recycle a bulky item I cannot transport?
Check with your local authority - they should be able to arrange a bulky waste collection for you. Contact us @ info@howtorecycle.ng, we’ll help you out with this.
Can I schedule a pick-up on the app?
Currently, you can contact the nearest recycling centre to inquire about scheduling a pickup. We are actively developing a feature within our app that will allow you to schedule pickups directly.
