General (how to recycle at home):
  • Collect your recyclable materials in a designated bin or receptables. This can be anything from cardboard boxes and paper to plastic bottles and aluminium cans.
  • Sort your recyclables into the correct categories based on material type. This will help the recycling process go more smoothly and ensure that the materials are recycled properly.
  • Rinse and clean your recyclables before putting them in the bin. This will help reduce contamination and make sure the materials are recycled correctly.
  • Make sure to recycle every type of recyclable material separately. This includes paper, plastic, metal, flexibles, used beverage cartons and glass.
  • Try to avoid creating excess waste in the first place. This will help reduce the number of recyclable materials that need to be processed.
